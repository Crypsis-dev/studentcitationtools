About APA 7th Edition

APA (American Psychological Association) style is commonly used for citing sources in psychology, education, and social sciences.

Key changes in 7th edition:

  • Up to 20 authors listed in reference entries
  • DOIs and URLs formatted as hyperlinks
  • Simplified in-text citations for 3+ authors
  • New guidelines for online sources
