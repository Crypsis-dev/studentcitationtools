About APA 7th Edition Format

APA (American Psychological Association) style is the standard format used in psychology, education, social sciences, and many other academic disciplines. Our tool follows the latest APA 7th edition guidelines.

üìö Book Citation Format

Author, A. A. (Year). Title of work. Publisher.

Example: Smith, J. A., & Johnson, M. L. (2023). Psychology of Modern Learning. Academic Press.

üì∞ Journal Article Format

Author, A. A. (Year). Article title. Journal Title, Volume(Issue), pages. DOI

Example: Brown, K. L. (2024). Cognitive development in adolescents. Journal of Education, 45(2), 123-145.
