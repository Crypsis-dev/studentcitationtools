Citation Examples

APA Book

Input: Author: Kahneman, Daniel; Year: 2011; Title: Thinking, Fast and Slow; Publisher: Farrar, Straus and Giroux

Output: Kahneman, D. (2011). Thinking, fast and slow. Farrar, Straus and Giroux.

MLA Journal

Input: Author: Smith, John; Year: 2023; Title: Modern Education; Journal: Education Today; Volume: 45; Issue: 2

Output: Smith, John. "Modern Education." Education Today, vol. 45, no. 2, 2023, pp. 123-145.

Chicago Website

Input: Author: World Health Organization; Year: 2023; Title: Health Report; URL: who.int/report

Output: World Health Organization. "Health Report." Last modified 2023. https://who.int/report.
