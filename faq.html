General Questions

What is StudentCitationTools.com?

StudentCitationTools.com is a free online citation generator that helps students, researchers, and writers create accurate citations in APA, MLA, and Chicago styles. Our tool simplifies the citation process and saves time on academic writing.

Is StudentCitationTools.com really free?

Yes! Our core citation generator is 100% free with no hidden costs. We offer Premium features for power users who need advanced functionality like batch generation and plagiarism checking, but the basic citation generator will always be free.

Who can use this tool?

Our tool is designed for:

  • Students: High school, college, and university students
  • Researchers: Academic and professional researchers
  • Writers: Authors, journalists, and content creators
  • Educators: Teachers and professors
  • Anyone who needs to create citations

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required for the free version. You can generate citations immediately without any registration. Premium features require an account for subscription management and feature access.

Citation Questions

Which citation styles do you support?

We support the three most popular academic citation styles:

  • APA 7th Edition: American Psychological Association style
  • MLA 9th Edition: Modern Language Association style
  • Chicago 17th Edition: Chicago Manual of Style

These styles cover 95% of academic citation needs across various disciplines.

How accurate are your citations?

Our citations are generated based on the official style guides for each citation format. We regularly update our algorithms to match the latest editions and guidelines.

Important: While we strive for 100% accuracy, always double-check citations against your institution's specific requirements. Some departments or professors may have unique formatting rules.

How does ISBN lookup work?

Our ISBN lookup feature automatically retrieves book information by ISBN (International Standard Book Number). Here's how it works:

  1. Enter a valid ISBN-10 or ISBN-13 in the search field
  2. Our system queries multiple book databases
  3. We retrieve author, title, publisher, publication date, and other details
  4. The information automatically fills your citation form
  5. You can then generate the citation with one click

What source types can I cite?

We support citations for various source types including:

  • Books and e-books
  • Journal articles
  • Websites and web pages
  • Newspapers and magazines
  • Theses and dissertations
  • Conference papers
  • Videos and films
  • Social media posts
