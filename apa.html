APA 7th Edition Formatting Rules

Book Citations

Format: Author, A. A. (Year). Title of work. Publisher.

Example: Smith, J. A. (2023). Psychology of learning. Academic Press.

Journal Article Citations

Format: Author, A. A. (Year). Article title. Journal Title, Volume(Issue), pages. DOI

Example: Johnson, M. L., & Brown, K. (2024). Cognitive development. Journal of Education, 45(2), 123-145. https://doi.org/10.xxxx

Website Citations

Format: Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day). Title of page. Site Name. URL

Example: World Health Organization. (2023, March 15). Mental health report. WHO. https://who.int/report
