 APA 7th Edition Quick Guide

                
                
 Basic Reference Format

                

                    Author, A. A. (Year). Title of work: Subtitle if any. Publisher.
                


                
 Journal Article Format

                
Format: Author, A. A. (Year). Title of article. Journal Name, Volume(Issue), pages. DOI or URL

                

                    Smith, J. D., & Johnson, M. L. (2023). Cognitive development in early childhood. 
                    Journal of Educational Psychology, 115(2), 245-267. 
                    https://doi.org/10.1037/edu0000456
                


                
 Book Format

                
Format: Author, A. A. (Year). Title of book: Subtitle if any (Edition). Publisher.

                

                    American Psychological Association. (2020). 
                    Publication manual of the American Psychological Association (7th ed.).
                


                
 Website Format

                
Format: Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day). Title of page. Website Name. URL

                

                    Centers for Disease Control and Prevention. (2023, March 15). 
                    Mental health statistics. https://www.cdc.gov/mentalhealth
                


                
 In-Text Citations

                

                    

                        
One author:

                        (Smith, 2023)
                    

                    

                        
Two authors:

                        (Smith & Johnson, 2023)
                    

                    

                        
Three or more authors:

                        (Smith et al., 2023)
                    

                    

                        
Direct quote with page:

                        (Smith, 2023, p. 45)
