Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Is StudentCitationTools.com really free?

Yes! Our core citation generator is 100% free with no hidden costs. We also offer Premium features for power users who need advanced functionality.

Do you store my data?

No. All citation generation happens locally in your browser. We don't store any of your citation data unless you explicitly save it (and even then, it's stored locally on your device).

Can I use this tool on mobile?

Absolutely! Our website is fully responsive and works perfectly on all devices including smartphones and tablets. No app download required.

Which citation styles do you support?

We support APA 7th edition, MLA 9th edition, and Chicago 17th edition - the three most popular academic styles. These cover 95% of academic citation needs.

How does the ISBN lookup work?

Our tool queries book databases using the ISBN you provide and automatically retrieves author, title, publisher, year, and other details to fill your citation form automatically.

What's included in Premium?

Premium includes unlimited citations, batch generation, advanced export formats, citation history, plagiarism check, and ad-free experience. See all features.
