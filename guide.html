üìò APA 7th Edition Formatting Guide

Used in: Psychology, Education, Social Sciences

Paper Format

  • Font: 12-point Times New Roman
  • Margins: 1 inch on all sides
  • Spacing: Double-spaced throughout
  • Page Header: Running head (short title) + page number
  • Title Page: Required with specific elements

In-Text Citations

One author: (Smith, 2024)

Two authors: (Smith & Johnson, 2024)

Three or more: (Smith et al., 2024)

Direct quote: (Smith, 2024, p. 45)

Reference List

  • Title: "References" centered at top
  • Alphabetical by author's last name
  • Hanging indent (0.5 inch)
  • Include DOI or URL when available
  • Italicize titles of books and journals

üìó MLA 9th Edition Formatting Guide

Used in: Humanities, Literature, Arts

Paper Format

  • Font: 12-point Times New Roman
  • Margins: 1 inch on all sides
  • Spacing: Double-spaced
  • Header: Last name + page number (top right)
  • Heading: Your name, instructor, course, date (left aligned)
  • Title: Centered, not bold or underlined

In-Text Citations

Basic format: (Author Page)

Example: (Smith 45)

No author: ("Title" Page) or (Shortened Title Page)

Multiple authors: (Smith and Johnson 45) or (Smith et al. 45)

Works Cited Page

  • Title: "Works Cited" centered at top
  • Alphabetical by author's last name
  • Hanging indent (0.5 inch)
  • Italicize titles of books and containers
  • Use quotation marks for articles and chapters

üìï Chicago 17th Edition Formatting Guide

Two Systems: Notes-Bibliography (humanities) & Author-Date (sciences)

Notes-Bibliography System

Used in: History, Literature, Arts

  • Footnotes: Superscript numbers in text
  • First citation: Full citation in note
  • Subsequent citations: Short form
  • Bibliography: Alphabetical list at end

Author-Date System

Used in: Sciences, Social Sciences

  • In-text: (Author Year, Page)
  • Example: (Smith 2024, 45)
  • Reference list: Similar to APA

Paper Format

  • Font: 12-point Times New Roman or similar
  • Margins: 1 inch minimum
  • Spacing: Double-spaced main text
  • Notes: Single-spaced with blank line between
  • Page numbers: Top right or centered bottom
